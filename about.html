MO is an ethical memories network started in Ireland.

MO founders Rishabh and Pablo met in 2018 close to St. Patrick's cathedral in Dublin, Ireland. They became friends and started working to create a humane platform they would trust with their most precious memories. They saw that there was still no digital replacement for the old family photobook collection. They also wanted a humane alternative to the typical social networks. After iterating different network designs and business models, they had a breakthrough in 2021. In 2022 they built it, and now you can start using it.

MO is an ethical organization. We design, build, maintain, and improve humane IT products, and we are carbon neutral.
Anyone can join a memory. Anyone can upload unlimited photos and videos to the memory. Photos and videos are stored in their original quality for free forever.
Only members can create new memories. New accounts have a 6-month free membership. The Membership is €3 per month). More info in our Terms and Conditions
