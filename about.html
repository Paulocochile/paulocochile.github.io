MO is a memories network managed by a nonprofit foundation based in Ireland.

MO founders Pablo (From the Chilean Andes) and Rishabh (From the Indian Himalayas) met close in St. Patricks church in Ireland in around 2018. They quickly became friends and decided to create a humane platform where they would be comfortable with storing and sharing their most precious and intimate memories, creating a humane alternative to social media.
After years of prototyping and iterating with different designs and business models they finally got it.
In order to be a reliable, sustainable, ethical memories network, Memory Oak is controlled by a nonprofit organization/foundation. This is the only way to ensure our principles are maintained in the long term, hence creating a place we can trust with our most precious memories. Our organization's mission is to provide ethical IT products that are made to improve people's lives. We strive to build, maintain, and restore the public’s trust in IT products. People can upload unlimited photos and videos for free thanks to the monthly donations provided by our community. All of our revenue goes towards creating, maintaining and improving our products.
Anyone with a MO account can join a memory. People in a memory can upload unlimited pictures and photos to it. These photos and videos are stored in their original quality for free forever. New accounts have a X-month trial where they can create memories, after the trial expires you need to become a member to create more memories.
All gains are reinvested in developing Accessible and Humane IT Products (MO is controlled by a non profit organization)
