MO is a memories network managed by a nonprofit foundation based in Ireland.

MO founders Rishabh and Pablo met in 2018 close to St. Patrick's cathedral in Dublin, Ireland. They became friends and started working to create a humane platform they would trust with their most precious memories. They saw that there was still no digital replacement for the old family photobook collection. They also wanted a humane alternative to the typical social networks. After iterating different network designs and business models, they had a breakthrough in 2021. In 2022 they built it, and now you can start using it.

A charitable organization controls Memory Oak so that it can remain a reliable, sustainable, and ethical memories network. Our organization's mission is to provide ethical IT products that are made to improve people's lives. All of our revenue goes towards creating, maintaining and improving our products. Anyone with a MO account can join a memory. Anyone in a memory can upload unlimited pictures and photos to it. Photos and videos are stored in their original quality for free forever. Memories are deleted if everyone leaves them.
New accounts have a 6-month free membership where they can create new memories. When this expires you need to become a member for €1 per month in order to create more memories.
